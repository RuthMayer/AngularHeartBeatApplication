- A bit about HeartBeat -


The main idea of our project is saving valuable time for doctors.

A doctor spends most of his working day questioning his patients with the relevant questions to his/her condition to receive a detailed diagnose of the patient's condition and take care of him/her accordingly.

We focused on the cardiac property and created a chatbot called 'Heart Beat'.

The bot questions a potential cardiac patient with questions about symptoms that might lead to heart disease.


The main symptoms:

  • Blood pressure level.
  • History of heart diseases or heart attack in the past etc.
  • If the patient is suffering or have suffered in the past from diabetes.
  • Level of cholesterol.
  • Exercise habits of patient.
  • Smoking habits of patients.
  • Pills or medications the patient takes regularly.

After the above questions are answered by the patient, We create a very clear form divided into sections, with all the information in it.

Each form receives a 'score' of urgency given to it by a built algorithm that calculates all the answered symptoms together, Say if the patient is smoking he will get a higher score than one who is not smoking.

Eventually, when the form is filled the doctor will receive an email with it and there will be shown to the doc a website with all the requests, ordered by their score. So he could see more urgent cases first.
